COMPANY MEMBERS
Legend Books & Publishing was created by Kimberly Nguyen and Jamie
Chastain. Both are avid readers, but felt there was little
representation for People of Color, LGBTQ+, and other minority
groups in commercially published books. Legend Books & Publishing
aims to give a safe space for such authors to tell their stories.
